Recipe #1

Ingredient Amount
Flour 1 cup
Milk 1 cup
Egg 1
Sugar 2 tbsp

Steps

  1. Mix all dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Whisk in milk and egg until smooth.
  3. Cook small circles of batter on a hot pan until golden.

Recipe #2

Ingredient Amount
Boiled potatoes 3
Boiled eggs 2
Boiled carrots 1
Mayonnaise 1 cup
Pickles 2-3

Steps

  1. Boil potatoes, carrots and eggs, then cool them.
  2. Dice vegetables, eggs and pickles into small cubes.
  3. Mix everything with peas and mayonnaise, add salt and pepper.
  4. Chill in the fridge before serving.
